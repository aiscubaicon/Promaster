Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our diving courses

What is the minimum age for diving courses?

The minimum age for PADI Open Water Diver course is 10 years old. Junior Open Water Divers (ages 10-14) must dive with a PADI Professional or certified parent/guardian and are limited to maximum depths of 12 meters.

Do I need to know how to swim?

Yes, basic swimming skills are required. You need to be able to swim at least 200 meters (any stroke) and tread water for 10 minutes. Don't worry if you're not an Olympic swimmer - basic comfort in water is sufficient.

What's included in the course price?

All course prices include instruction, digital learning materials, use of all scuba equipment during training, certification processing fees, and refreshments during breaks. Accommodation and meals are not included unless specified.

How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies: Open Water (3-4 days), Advanced Open Water (2-3 days), Rescue Diver (3-4 days), and specialty courses (1-2 days). We can adjust schedules to fit your vacation timeframe.

Can I start my course online?

Yes! PADI eLearning allows you to complete the knowledge development portion online before your trip. This saves time and lets you focus on the fun parts - pool and open water training.
