About This Dive Site

Giftun Island is one of the most popular diving destinations in the Red Sea, and for good reason. This protected marine park offers some of the most pristine coral reefs in the region, with crystal-clear waters that provide excellent visibility year-round.

The island features multiple dive sites suitable for all levels, from shallow coral gardens perfect for beginners to deeper walls that challenge more experienced divers. The biodiversity here is exceptional, with over 200 species of coral and countless varieties of tropical fish.

What makes Giftun Island special is its protected status, which has allowed the marine ecosystem to flourish. You'll encounter healthy coral formations, schools of colorful reef fish, and if you're lucky, larger marine life including rays, turtles, and reef sharks.

The dive sites around Giftun Island offer something for everyone. The shallow lagoons are perfect for snorkeling and beginner divers, while the outer reefs provide thrilling drift dives with excellent coral formations and marine life encounters.

What to Expect

Your day at Giftun Island begins with a comfortable boat ride from Hurghada Marina. The journey takes approximately 45 minutes, during which you can enjoy the beautiful Red Sea scenery and prepare for your dives.

We typically conduct two dives at different sites around the island, allowing you to experience the variety of underwater landscapes. Between dives, you'll have time to relax on the boat, enjoy a delicious lunch, and share your underwater experiences with fellow divers.
